From our design jam today - brief overview of what the MVP contains to discuss at this weeks meeting with lecturers:
• Players can move, interact with ‘activities’ located directly in front of them
• Cannons can be loaded, fired, chance of breaking on fire, recoil on fire, can be upgraded
• Whale can cause wave pushing players off deck
• Ship must be steered to avoid obstacle (camera pan)
• If damaged, the deck floods, move slower, player can bail this water
• Enemy ships, indicated with flag, player must time cannon shot
• Enemy ships fire on deck, persistent damage, block path
• Crows nest used to telegraph
• Ship upgrades / different ships / different cannons/hull
• Resources
• Rewards – layered objectives
• POSSIBLY negative feedback – PROBABLY positive feedback
